Emergency Procedures

Step-by-step guide for different emergencies

Fire Emergency

  1. Shout "Fire!" to alert everyone
  2. Call 911 or fire department
  3. Use stairs, not elevators
  4. Crawl low under smoke

Medical Emergency

  1. Ensure area is safe
  2. Call 911 immediately
  3. Provide basic care if trained
  4. Clear path for responders

Earthquake

  1. DROP to the ground
  2. COVER under sturdy furniture
  3. HOLD ON until shaking stops
  4. Evacuate if necessary

Typhoon/Flood

  1. Stay updated on warnings
  2. Move to higher ground if flooding
  3. Avoid flood waters
  4. Have emergency kit ready

Emergency Preparedness Kit

Essential items to prepare for emergencies

Medical & First Aid

  • Bandages and antiseptic
  • Prescription medications
  • Pain relievers
  • Medical gloves

Food & Water

  • 3-day water supply (1 gallon/person/day)
  • Non-perishable food
  • Manual can opener
  • Energy bars

Light & Communication

  • Flashlight with extra batteries
  • Portable phone charger
  • Battery-powered radio
  • Whistle

Important Documents

  • Copies of IDs in waterproof container
  • Insurance papers
  • Medical records
  • Emergency contact list

Important Tips:

  • Store kit in accessible location
  • Check and update every 6 months
  • Have one kit at home and one in car
  • Include special needs items (baby, elderly, pets)
